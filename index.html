Join us November 12-19, 2014

Requirements

  • Twitter™

You need a Twitter™ account to participate. Since Twitter™ may not be available on Thales corporate network, you are invited to use your personal devices.

  • Location

This event support geo-referenced tweets. If you want to share your location, please do so by enabling such functionality on your devices. Otherwise, we'll try to infer your location to place your tweets on the map.

  • Chrome™ or Firefox™.
  • WebGL

On Nov 19th 2014, we will share a link to a geo-sentiment thematic map. If you want to see the opinions of your peers on several questions, follow the link and make sure to use one of our preferred internet browser.

How to participate

Join in using Twitter hash (#thalesgisday) between November 12th-19th and find out more about the opinions of colleagues across the globe.

  • Answer question by adding the following hashes: #thalesgisday and the question number
    Ex.: I completely agree to #Q1 at #thalesgisday. This technology is awesome!
  • We do support media: photos, video, etc. However, sentiment analysis will only be performed on your 144 english characters!
